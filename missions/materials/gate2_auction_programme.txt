You are invited to attend a private sale of a single extraordinary
and unrepeatable asset. The nature of the lot has been disclosed
to you in advance under separate cover.

Attendance is by personal invitation only. No guests. No recording.
No disclosure of attendance or proceedings.

Please confirm your attendance no later than 48 hours prior via
the dedicated contact number provided to you at the time of
your original approach.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SCHEDULE OF EVENTS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  19:00     ARRIVAL & REGISTRATION
            Pre-Function Foyer, Hermitage Event Level.
            Credential verification and NDA execution.
            Escorted entry only. No unescorted movement.

  19:30     LOT PREVIEW OPENS
            Lot Viewing Salon, Salon B suite.
            The asset (physical representation only; digital
            transfer secured separately) will be available
            for inspection.
            Representatives of the consignor will be present
            to answer technical questions.

  20:45     LOT PREVIEW CLOSES
            The viewing salon will be cleared.
            Attendees should proceed to the reception
            holding area on the Event Level.
            Light refreshments will be served.

            ┄┄┄ [Staff and logistics reposition] ┄┄┄

  21:05     BIDDING SESSION OPENS
            Main Bidding Room, Salon B suite.
            Single-lot sealed bid. Final offers submitted
            in writing to Maître Renoux personally.
            No electronic devices permitted in the room.

  21:30     BIDDING CLOSES
            Bids reviewed privately.
            Winning bid confirmed to all attendees
            by 21:45 at the latest.

  22:00     POST-AWARD DOCUMENTATION
            Winning bidder proceeds with legal counsel
            to the documentation suite adjacent to
            Vault Corridor B.
            Transfer protocol initiated.

  22:30     ASSET TRANSFER
            Secured courier handover via designated
            back-of-house route.
            All other attendees escorted to exit from
            the Main Bidding Room.
